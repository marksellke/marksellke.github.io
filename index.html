CV

I am a member at IAS. My interests include probability, statistics, optimization, and machine learning. In Fall 2023, I will join the Department of Statistics at Harvard University as an assistant professor.

I received my Ph.D. in mathematics from Stanford, advised by Andrea Montanari and Sébastien Bubeck. Previously I studied at MIT and the University of Cambridge.
